## Decision: Ready or Not?

### If You're Scoring ≥ 80% on Practice Questions

!!! success "Ready to Schedule"
    You're likely prepared. Recommended final steps:

    1. Take one final mock exam under timed conditions
    2. Review any missed questions
    3. Quick flashcard review the night before
    4. Schedule with confidence

### If You're Scoring 60-80%

!!! warning "Almost Ready — Focus on Gaps"
    You need targeted study. Follow this plan:

    1. Identify which domains you're weakest in
    2. Re-read relevant study notes sections
    3. Practice domain-specific questions
    4. Retake mock exam after 2-3 days of focused study
    5. Schedule when consistently above 80%

### If You're Scoring Below 60%

!!! danger "Not Ready — Structured Study Needed"
    Recommended remediation plan:

    | Day | Focus | Activity |
    |-----|-------|----------|
    | 1-2 | Domain 2 (highest weight) | Study notes + practice |
    | 3 | Domain 4 (Security) | Study notes + practice |
    | 4 | Domain 5 (Collaboration) | Study notes + practice |
    | 5 | Domain 1 (Architecture) | Study notes + practice |
    | 6 | Domain 3 & 6 | Study notes + practice |
    | 7 | All domains | Mock exam + review |
    | 8 | Weak areas | Targeted review |
    | 9 | Final review | Flashcards + cheat sheets |
    | 10 | Rest | Light review only |

## Exam Day Tips

!!! tip "On Exam Day"
    - Read each question completely before looking at answers
    - For scenario questions: identify the domains being tested first
    - Eliminate obviously wrong answers (security violations, full autonomy for production)
    - When two answers seem correct, choose the more comprehensive one
    - Flag difficult questions and return to them
    - Watch the timer — don't spend > 3 minutes on any single question
    - Trust your preparation!
